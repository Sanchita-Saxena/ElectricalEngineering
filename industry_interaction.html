Industry Institute Partnership Cell

IIPC - Industry Institute Partnership Cell

The Industry-Institute Partnership Cell (IIPC) of DEI was established in 2002 with sponsorship from AICTE with a view to forge meaningful bonds with industry. The Cell apart from providing consultancy and testing facilities to different industrial houses, helps to organize short-term courses, seminars and lectures by eminent industry persons at the Institute level. As a result of endeavors of IIPC, the quality and level of the student placements from the Institute has got a tremendous fillip in its short span of existence. It is envisaged that IIPC will further develop meaningful linkages with industry through mutually beneficial collaborations.

The activities undertaken by IIPC include:

  • Providing consultancy in the area of Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Computers, Mechanics and Civil Engineering, Heat Engines and Workshop.
  • Organizing Short-Term Courses in the fields of Electronics, Microprocessors, Management and Computers.
  • Coordinating with the Placement Cell.
  • Arranging Summer Training of the students of various faculties.
  • Conducting Workshops on Resume writing, Facing Interviews and Group Discussions.
  • Coordinating activities related to entrepreneurship development and Campus Connect Programme.

Campus Connect Programme

The Institute has signed an MOU with M/s Infosys, a leading software company of India, under which both DEI and Infosys will work together for mutual benefit. The activities include:

  • Running of computer science oriented courses designed by Infosys in DEI campus.
  • Exchange of faculty/ industry experts under sabbatical.
  • Use of infrastructural facilities of Infosys by the students and staff of DEI.
  • Facilitation of student project work.
