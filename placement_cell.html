Training and Placement Cell

The Faculty Training and Placement Cell (FTPC) co-ordinates with various industries and organizations for campus recruitment. The FTPC comprises four faculty members and a group of twelve Student Representatives from various classes. The FTPC also coordinates with the placement cell of the DEI Technical College for placement of diploma students.

The FTPC is further supported by the institute's Industry-Institute Partnership Cell (IIPC) together with the DEI Alumni Placement Assistance Cell (DEIAPAC).
