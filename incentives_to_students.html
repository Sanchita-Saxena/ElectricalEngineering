Incentives to the Students for Achieving Excellence

The faculty has evolved a system of awards which provide incentives to the students to achieve excellence in their endeavors. These award give Institute-wide recognition to the deserving students. The awards are:

  • Founder's Medal for the best all-round performance.
  • President's Medal for best academic performance in the Institute.
  • Director's Medal for Final Year Toppers.
  • Director's Medal for best performance in Cultural Education and Comparative Study of Religion .
  • Prof V. G. Sastry Memorial Awards for General Proficiency in each class.
  • Prizes for Toppers of each Class.
  • Prizes for extra curricular activities-Literary, sports, cultural etc.
  • Certificates for various student bodies.
