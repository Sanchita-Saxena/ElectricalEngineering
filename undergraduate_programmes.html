B.Tech in Electrical Engineering

The U.G. Programme in Electrical Engineering is broad based; however apart from offering broad based degree in Electrical Engineering the departmet also offers following specialization (optional) :

  • B.Tech. in Electrical with specialization in Electronics & Communication
  • B.Tech. in Electrical with specialization in Computer Science

The objective is to encourage students who wish to specialize in an area.
