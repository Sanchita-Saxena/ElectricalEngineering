Students’ Chapters of Institution of Engineers

The Students’ Chapters (Electrical & Mechanical) Institution of Engineers (India), Faculty of Engineering, was established on 01/03/1979 for inculcating in the students a feeling of belongingness to the fraternity of Engineers. The students’ chapters organize various activities like special lectures, Quiz competitions, Group Discussions, Seminars, Workshops, Symposia, etc. for the development of socio-techno-intellectual qualities in the students as well as for encouraging their quest for knowledge in a disciplined manner.

Now student’s Chapter is available for all engineering branches, namely:

  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Footwear Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Agriculture Engineering
